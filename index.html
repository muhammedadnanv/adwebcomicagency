F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Ad Web Comic Agency offer?

We offer a wide range of services including website development, content writing, social media handling, UI/UX design for websites and apps, and web page writing.

How can I get a quote for my project?

You can get a quote by contacting us through our website or by reaching out to us via email or phone. Provide us with the details of your project, and we will get back to you with a customized quote.

What is the typical turnaround time for a project?

The turnaround time varies depending on the scope and complexity of the project. Typically, smaller projects can be completed within a week, while larger projects may take several weeks. We work closely with our clients to meet deadlines.

Do you offer support after the project is completed?

Yes, we provide ongoing support and maintenance for all our projects. We ensure that your website or application runs smoothly after launch, and we offer various support packages to meet your needs.

Can I request revisions during the project?

Absolutely! We value your feedback and allow revisions during the project to ensure the final product meets your expectations. We will work with you to make any necessary adjustments.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit cards, and PayPal. We also offer flexible payment plans depending on the size and duration of the project.
